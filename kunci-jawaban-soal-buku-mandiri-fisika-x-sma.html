5. OK Google kunci jawaban untuk kelas 8 buku mandiri bahasa Inggris kurikulum 2013 halaman 106 1practice 2​


Change the verbs in brackets into their past form.

Choosing the appropriate word to fill the blanks is not too difficult if we've known the meaning of each given words.

Based on the text, it is talking about the last school vacation and we will complete the text with the form of past tense.

The simple past tense, sometimes called the preterite, is used to talk about a completed action in a time before now. The time of the action can be in the recent past or the distant past and action duration is not important.

We always use the simple past when we say when something happened, so it is associated with certain past time expressions.

To understand more, let's discuss it.

PEMBAHASAN :

Aldo and his family VISITED Karimun Jawa on the last school vacation. It was a short visit. Early in the morning, they WENT snorkeling. A guide HELPED the family enjoy the activities. They ENJOYED the beautiful reef and the fish in the water. After that they visited the shark-breeding pool. They HAD a chance to swim with the sharks. It was scary. Aldo TREMBLED with fear when he WATCHED the sharks swimming around him and his family. Then, soon he LEFT the shark pool and enjoyed playing with other sea creatures, such as blowfish, starfish, and turtles. The blowfish PUFFED itself up when Ivan HELD it on his hand.

40. Tolong yang punya buku mandiri iPA kelas 9 mandiri Erlangga tolong fotoin soal tentang listrik statis​


Berikut ini adalah latihan soal IPA Listrik Statis kelas 9 SMP dilengkapi kunci jawabannya. Soal berbentuk pilihan ganda berjumlah 30 butir soal dengan tiap-tiap soal diberi kunci jawabannya.

Soal nomor 1

Partikel terkecil penyusun suatu materi atau zat disebut ….

A. elektron

B. atom

C. neutron

D. proton

Kunci jawaban : B

Bunyi ledakan yang menggelegar akibat pemuaian udara secara tiba-tiba disebut ….

A. petir

B. angin

C. guntur

D. listrik

Kunci jawaban : A

Soal nomor 3

Bagian atom yang bermuatan positif adalah ……

A. proton

B. neutron

C. elektron

D. inti

Kunci jawaban : A

Soal nomor 4

Pada benda yang bermuatan listrik negatif keadaannya adalah …….

A. jumlah elektron > Jumlah proton

B. jumlah elektron < Jumlah proton

C. jumlah elektron = Jumlah proton

D. tidak memiliki proton

Soal nomor 5

A. negatif

B. positif

C. netral

D. positron

A. proton dan elektron
