14. what do you have to do first when you want to use a fax machine​


Jawaban:

1. Make sure the fax machine is plugged into a power source and also plugged into a working phone jack.

2. Turn the fax machine on.

Obtain the fax number of the destination fax machine.

3. Gather the documents you want to send and put them in the order you want them to be received.

4. Fill out a separate piece of paper called a coversheet with the recipient's name, fax number/phone number, your name, your phone number, a short message and number of pages (including coversheet).

5. Lay the documents face-up in the fax machine feeder tray with the coversheet on top

6. Dial the recipient's fax number (dialing instructions for international calls)

7. Press the "fax" or "send" button, depending on the particular fax machine model

Jawaban:

1. First of all, prepare the document you want

to fax and make sure the destination number is

correct. If so, then the document is inserted into

the fax machine feeder. Place the sheet

precisely and straight, not at an angle.

2. After the paper is in the right position, press

the numeric buttons on the fax machine

(such as telephone numbers) according to the

destination number. Don't get it wrong

because it will be difficult to tell if you send

it wrong. After pressing the destination number

on the fax machine, next you only need to

press the send button. The size of this button

is usually slightly larger than other buttons.

3. Then the sheet of paper will be pulled in by

the fax machine feeder slowly, indicating

the fax process is in progress. After the fax

is complete, you will receive a confirmation

whether your document was sent successfully

or not.

29. To be First Mover in a business, you do not have to have market analysis. Do you agree or disagree to the statement?


I disagree with the statement that to be the first mover in a business you don’t have to have market analysis. Because to be the first mover you have to survey what the market needs exactly and what kind of stuff they demand. It’s because the market and business depends on supply and demand. No supply without demand. That’s why you have to have market analysis.

Pembahasan

Pertanyaan di atas artinya:

Untuk menjadi pelopor pertama dalam suatu usaha, anda tidak perlu melakukan analisis pasar.

Pernyataan ini harus kita tanggapi sesuai dengan pendapat atau sesuai dengan apa yang telah kita pelajari sebelumnya.

Jadi menurut pernyataan di atas untuk menjadi pelopor dalam suatu bidang usaha kita tidak perlu membuat analisis pasar. Pernyataan ini bisa keliru karena untuk menjadi pelopor usaha justru kita harus tahu apa yang sebenarnya dibutuhkan oleh pasar lalu memenuhinya sebelum celah tersebut diisi oleh orang lain. Seperti kita tahu untuk menjadi sukses dalam bisnis kita harus memperhatikan supply and demand, yaitu pasokan dan permintaan. Bila kita tahu apa yang diminta pasar (demand) maka kita bisa memasok (supply) barang atau jasa tersebut.

Hal lain yang termasuk analisis adalah bagaimana pendapat pasar tentang barang yang kita sediakan sehingga kita bisa memperbaiki kualitas atau kuantitas barang kita.

36. Aboriginal Australian (think) to have first arrived on the Australian mainland by boat. UBAH MENJADI PAST TENSE​


Jawabannya adalah menggunakan Verb-2 dari think yaitu THOUGHT ✅

=> Aboriginal Australian THOUGHT to have first arrived on the Australian mainland by boat.

PEMBAHASAN Simple Past Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yang terjadi di masa lalu atau Peristiwa yang ada pada masa lampau.

Rumus Past Tense

(+) S + V2 + O/C

(-) S + did not + V1 + O/C

(?) Did + S + V1 + O/C?

Catatan:Menggunakan Verb-2 pada kalimat Positive Tapi menggunakan Verb-1 pada kalimat Negative dan Interrogative

KESIMPULAN

Aboriginal Australian (think) to have first arrived on the Australian mainland by boat.

➡️ Aboriginal Australian thought to have first arrived on the Australian mainland by boat.

Keterangan:Verb-1 => think Verb-2 => thought ✅

Modals - 'must' and 'mustn't'

Do you want to practise using 'must' and 'mustn't' in English? Play our grammar games and have fun while you learn.

Help

Grammar Rule

Examples

I must do my homework.

You mustn’t be late for class.

We must wear our school uniform.

Remember!

We can use ‘must’ to talk about rules. For the negative, we can say ‘must not’ or ‘mustn’t’.

Parents must take care of their children.

You mustn’t shout in the library.

She must not forget her books.

Be careful!

The main verb is without ‘to’.

He must be careful when cutting food.

I mustn’t make a mess.

We say... We don’t say...

You must look both ways when crossing the road. (NOT You must to look both ways when crossing the road.)

We mustn’t run in school. (NOT We mustn’t to run in school.)

Children must respect their parents. (NOT Children must respecting their parents.)
