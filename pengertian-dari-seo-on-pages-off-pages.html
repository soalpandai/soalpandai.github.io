14. Claire reads 1⁄2 of a book on Monday ,She reads 2/3 of the remainder on Tuesday .If she had 38 pages more pages to read,how many pages were there in the book ?


Total pages of the book = ....?

On Monday she read 1/2 of the book
1 - 1/2 = 1/2
So, she had 1/2 of the book to read

On Tuesday she read 2/3 of the remaining
1/2 x 2/3 = 1/3
1/2 - 1/3 = 3/6 - 2/6 = 1/6
So, she had 1/6 of the book to read

If she had 38 pages more to read, how many pages were there in the book?
(TP = total pages of the book)
1/6 TP = 38
TP = 38 x 6
TP = 228

So, there were 228 pages were there in the book

40. Ray read 1/4of a story book on Monday and 1/5 of it on Tuesday.There are 80 pages in the book.How many pages in total did he read on both days?​


Jawaban:

Ray read 32 pages in total.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

total pages= 80

1/4= 80÷1/4= 20

Monday= 20 pages

pages left= 80-20= 60 pages

Tuesday= 1/5

1/5= 60÷5= 12

Tuesday= 12 pages

total from both days= 20+12= 32 pages
