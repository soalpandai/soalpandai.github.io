Diketahui:

Skala (S)=1:700.000

Jarak Peta (JP)=20 cm

Ditanya:

Jarak Sebenarnya (JS)?

Dijawab:

JS=JP÷S

=20÷1/700.000

=20×700.000/1

=14.000.000 cm

=14.000.000÷100.000

=140 km

Kesimpulan:

Jadi,jarak sebenarnya dari tambun ke Bandung adalah 140 km

10. He and two ... suspects threatened the victim with a stun gun A. Others B. Another C. Other D. The other E. The others +alasannya


A. Others

Semoga membantu :)

Answer:

When we talk about the other people or other stuff, there are differences depending on its circumstance.
If you talk about other things or people but still within a group or category, you use
'another' for singular
'others' or 'other cars/ countries / players etc' for plural.
Meanwhile, for talking about the other people or stuff outside the category or group, you use
'the other' for singular
'the others' for plural.
Here is the example:
Messi and Aguero have always been playing in each game while other strikers like Higuain and Dybala have barely played for Argentina in this world cup. (we use 'others / other strikers ' because they are still within the Argentina squad for the world cup)
Beside those four, the other promising strikers are Icardi, Angel Correa and Giovanni Simeone. Unfortunately, they weren't picked for the squad. (we use 'the others/ the other strikers' as they're outside the squad)

The answer for the exercise above is
He and two other (c) suspects threatened the victim with stun gun. (because other suspects are still included in that group of criminal).
