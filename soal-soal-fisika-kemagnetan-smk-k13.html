25. Ada yang bisa bantu soal SMK


a = 102

Un = 34/6.561

[tex]Un \: = a. {r}^{n - 1} \\ \\ \frac{34}{6.561} = 102. \: {( \frac{1}{3}) }^{n - 1} \\ \\ \frac{34}{6.561} = 102. {( \frac{1}{3} })^{n} . ({ \frac{1}{3} })^{ - 1} \\ \\ \frac{34}{6.561} = 102 \times 3 \times {3}^{ - n} \\ \\ \frac{34}{6.561} = 306 \times {3}^{ - n} \\ \\ \frac{34}{6.561} \times \frac{1}{306} = {3}^{ - n} \\ \\ \frac{ \cancel{34} }{6.561} \times \frac{1}{ \cancel{306} \: {}^{9} } = {3}^{ - n} \\ \\ \frac{1}{6.561} \times \frac{1}{9} = {3}^{ - n} \\ \\ {3}^{ - 8} \times {3}^{ - 2} = {3}^{ - n} \\ \\ {3}^{ - 10} = {3}^{ - n} \\ \\ \\ \\ - n = - 10 \\ \\ n = 10[/tex]

40. bantu jawab kk soal fisika kelas 10 smk​

Soal Tipe A

massa benda tersebut adalah 3 kg

[tex]\Sigma F_x = ma\\F = ma\\15 = 5m\\m = 3 \; kg[/tex]

Soal Tipe B

JIka C :

massa = 500 gr = 0,5 kg

[tex]\Sigma F_y = 0\\ N - mg = 0\\N = mg\\N = 0,5(10)\\N = 5 \; N[/tex]

[tex]\Sigma F_x = ma\\F = ma\\a = \frac{20}{0,5}\\a = 40 \; m/s^2[/tex]

Jika D :

[tex]\Sigma F_x = ma\\F \cos 60 = ma\\20 \frac{1}{2} = ma\\a = \frac{10}{0,5}\\a = 20 m/s^2[/tex]
