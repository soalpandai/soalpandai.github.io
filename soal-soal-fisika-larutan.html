Ion Al³⁺ mengendap = ¹/₄ bagian

Ion Al³⁺ larut = ³/₄ bagian = ³/₄ x 0,01 M = ³/₄₀₀ M

Al(OH)₃ → Al³⁺₍aq₎ + 3OH⁻₍aq₎

Ksp Al(OH)₃ = [Al³⁺] [OH⁻]³
2,5.10⁻³³ = ³/₄₀₀ [OH⁻]³
[OH⁻]³ = (1000/3).10⁻³³ = 1/3 . 10⁻³⁰

[OH⁻] = ∛(1/3 . 10⁻³⁰)
          = (1/3)^¹/₃ . 10⁻¹⁰

pOH = -log [OH⁻]
         = 10 - 1/3 log 1/3
         = 10 + 1/3 log 3
         = 10 + 0,16
         = 10,16

pH = 14 - 10,16
